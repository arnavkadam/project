Cricket World Cup 2023



The ICC cricket world cup is a quadriennial tournament which will be hosted in India. The 2023 world cup will be the 13th edition of the One Day International tournament. The tournament begins October 5th and the championship match will be on November 19th. This format of the game is very competitive and will consist of 10 teams.

Qualified Teams

There are many teams that have tried to secure their spot in the 2023 World Cup, however only 10 teams managed to qualify.
  1. Afghanistan
  2. Australia
  3. Bangladesh
  4. England
  5. India
  6. Netherlands
  7. New Zealand
  8. Pakistan
  9. South Africa
  10. Sri Lanka

Previous Champions

The previous edition of the cricket world cup was held in England, and after a nailbiting final, England were crowned champions of the world in 2019.
